Lockout Section

I acknowledge that lockout service may involve use of entry tools and that weather stripping, trim, glass, switches, interior handles, or locking systems may already be worn, damaged, or fragile. I am authorizing the use of these tools on my vehicle and understand the risks associated to using these tools such as scratches on the B pillar, torn weatherstripping, sprung doors, interior door scratches and anything else that could be associated to the use of such tools to perform the job.

Tire Service Section

I acknowledge that lug nuts, wheel studs, wheels, wheel covers, TPMS components, and related hardware may be rusted, swollen, seized, overtightened, cross-threaded, damaged, or otherwise compromised prior to service. Due to these conditions, removal of a wheel may require additional force or specialized tools and may result in further damage to already compromised components including lug nuts, studs, wheel covers, caps, or related hardware.

Chrome-capped lug nuts: Caps may be cracked, spinning, loose, or rusted underneath prior to service. These caps are fragile and may need to be removed during normal tire service. I accept full responsibility for any pre-existing or incidental damage to chrome caps.

I understand that roadside tire service is intended only to temporarily return the vehicle to a drivable condition so the vehicle may be moved to the nearest qualified tire repair facility. Temporary spare tires are typically designed for speeds not exceeding 50 mph and distances generally not exceeding approximately 40 miles from the location of installation, depending on the manufacturer specifications and the condition and age of the spare tire.

I acknowledge that spare tires deteriorate with age and that most temporary spares have a service life of approximately 4–6 years. I accept that Ozark Roadside, LLC, any contractors, dispatching platforms, including Agero and its parent companies, subsidiaries, affiliates, agents, representatives, and the technician cannot be responsible for the condition, age, prior use, or failure of a spare tire or any customer-supplied equipment used during the service.

If lug nuts or studs are broken, rounded, seized, cross-threaded, or otherwise unable to be removed using standard methods, I authorize the technician to use extractor tools or other necessary removal methods to remove the wheel and install the spare tire so the vehicle can be safely returned to the roadway. I understand that these procedures may cause additional damage to the affected lug nuts, studs, or related components and I authorize the technician to perform these procedures as necessary to complete the service.
